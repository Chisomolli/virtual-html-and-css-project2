Sir Pius Ufudo Complex

This is the flagship project of the Foundation. Located at St. Theresa’s Catholic Church, Nnobi, The Sir Pius Ufudo Complex is a modern

multi-use facility built by the Foundation and donated to the St Theresa’s Catholic Church, Nnobi. The complex has over 1,000m2 of

utility space and contains the following:

A nursery school and primary school · A library · Office facilities for Parish Priest, Catechist and teachers · Conference/ meeting rooms ·

Multipurpose 400-seater Hall · Modern toilets . Children’s playground

The School

Consisting of classrooms for nursery and primary

school students of St. Theresa’s Nursery & Primary

School, the Foundation has provided international-

standard infrastructure for the students as part of

playground and toilet facilities have also been

provided by the Foundation. This is a profound

departure from the current dilapidated make-shift

facilities of the School.

The Library

As part of the Sir Pius Ufudo House, the Foundation

has built an ultramodern Library. This is the first

fully furnished privately funded public library at

Nnobi. With over 10,000 books already purchased

by the Foundation, the library will be a center for

learning and academic research. It will be a melting

pot for academic interactions for the teeming

number of Nnobi students in secondary and

tertiary institutions across the country.

The Multipurpose Hall

The Lady Caroline Nkemakonam Ufudo

Multipurpose Hall & Event Center is an integral part

of the Sir Pius Ufudo House. The hall can seat up to

400 people (open style) or 250 people (banquet

table style) and is very suitable for conferences,

table style and is very suitable for conferences,

weddings, town-hall meetings, etc. Equipped with

audio and visual projection equipment, the hall is

also suitable for learning events, government and

community outreach programs and seminars. The

Hall is supported by a smaller 40-person seating

capacity conference room for smaller sized

meetings and events.
