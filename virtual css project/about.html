About Us

Who we are

We are a philanthropy focused on creating an

oasis of good health and improved

educational opportunities in our hometown,

Nnobi

The Sir Pius Ufudo Foundation was incorporated in 2018 as a not-for-profit

organization focused on the developing our hometown, Nnobi in Anambra State.

We seek to find sustainable and long-term solutions to some of the major

developmental issues facing our community and are working with like-minded

developmental issues facing our community and are working with like-minded

people and organisations to fund and implement the solutions.

We are building on the belief of our Patriarch, Late Sir Pius Ifeanyichukwu Ufudo,

that the surest path to societal development is through quality education

especially at primary and secondary school levels and adequate healthcare for

all.

Since inception, our social interventions in our community have been carefully

tailored toward making significant and long-term changes to the quality of life of

ndi Nnobi.

Our Mission

Providing access to quality education and

scholarship/financial support to the

underprivileged school children in Nnobi and

underprivileged school children in Nnobi and

other rural communities.

The Foundation over the years has encouraged differen

educational activities and has assisted many with scholarships.

The Foundation’s unflinching dedication to educational pursuits

has not gone unnoticed, and its tireless efforts in fostering

educational advancement have garnered much admiration and

respect
